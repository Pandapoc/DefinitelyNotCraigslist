Safety Tips

Although our site relies on the generosity and kindness of our users, we'd still highly recommend being careful when meeting someone for the first time. Here are some tips to help ensure that you have a pleasant and safe experience with others:
  • Only agree to meet in a public place that is well-lit and likely to have other people nearby.
  • Don't share any personal information like your address and phone number.
  • Let a friend or family member know what you're doing and where you're going.
  • Have a friend accompany you.
  • Always have your phone with you.
  • Trust your instincts; If you don't feel like the person you're meeting is safe or trustworthy, you can always cancel!
